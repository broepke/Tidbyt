Catfact

A random fact about a cat

Calls an external API and retrieves a random cat fact and renders it. Rotating every 4 minutes.

pixlet serve apps/catfact/catfact.star


pixlet check apps/catfact/catfact.star
pixlet format apps/catfact/catfact.star
pixlet lint --fix apps/catfact/catfact.star


pixlet render apps/catfact/catfact.star --gif --magnify 10
